First album is released in september

This is the first album, though the band has a long history

Although the band has a long history behind their backs, this is the first official album of the band under name "Aron's Land Cargo Co.". The band was called "No Man's Land" before.

That name was left behind because unfortunately it is not applicable to identify and distinguish the band. Also the band intends to leave behind some of its parts with the old name, while the new name still points out the essence of its music.

The new album brings the best of what the band has created so far. A soundtrack of the deserted lands of a man's life, and the tunes of his heart overflown with vibrant feelings.

No people can be as much in line as a band as these four brothers can.
